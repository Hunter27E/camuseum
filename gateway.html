Agreement & Description

Submission Fee

Non-refundable submission fee is $45 for submitting up to two (2) works. Entry fee is payable online by credit card or PayPal.

Agreement

Artists are responsible for printing their digital artworks and shipping them to and from the Museum. Shipping costs are carried by the artists. The Museum is not responsible for artwork in transit. Artwork abandoned at the museum after exhibition will be considered a donation and becomes possession of the Bolan Contemporary Art Museum. Entries that do not meet eligibility or artwork specifications criteria may not be judged or admitted to the exhibition.

Insurance, Publicity, Sales

While on premises, the Museum will insure work for the value given by the artist. For art valued at or over $1000, an appraisal may be required at the artist's expense. The Museum reserves the right to photograph and/or reproduce digital images of work for any related or educational purpose. The Museum is not a sales venue. Parties interested in purchasing work will be directed to the artist involved.

Benefits of Winning

Becoming a Bolan Contemporary Art Award Winner will boost your career by providing global media exposure, showing your work to an international audience via our website, newsletters, and social media network, and being ranked by our panel of industry-renowned jurors from all around the world. All exhibitionists will receive a Winner Certificate to promote their success. The mission of the Bolan Contemporary Art project is to raise awareness of local, national and international artwork and artists. Our goal is to excite, engage, and serve diverse communities through our collections, exhibitions, and educational programs. We connect generations of people to their cultural heritage and exceptional art. Our marketing team of dedicated art lovers and fans will promote your artwork and your artist personality the best way they can. Artists, who won the awards, will be asked to provide in formation and materials about their career, goals, photos of artwork, resumes or anything they choose for us to post on Social Media and in the Press.

Note: You will need a Google account to submit your artwork once the submission fee has been paid.
