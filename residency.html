Art Residency

Our art residency is very special to us. We are a very small community here and each member is an important and integral part of it. If you wish to reside in our building, please tell us what you wish to achieve while in Bolan. Would you coming by yourself or with your friends or family? Minimum stay is 1 month.

There is a full bathroom with shower and laundry facilities. The kitchen has refrigerator, oven/stove, microwave with cooking utensils and dishes. There are grants available. Please ask and we will provide you with more information.

Email us at mail@bolancontemporaryart.com.

Visit us at 4206 Tulip Lane, Bolan, IA 50448.
